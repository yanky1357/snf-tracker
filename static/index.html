Who Qualifies?

This program is available to New York Medicaid members who meet one or more of the following:

👩‍🐴

Pregnant or Postpartum

Women who are currently pregnant, recently had a miscarriage, or are within 12 months postpartum.

🏠

Health Home Enrolled

Individuals enrolled in a Health Home Care Management program.

👶

Children with Conditions

Families with children under 18 diagnosed with a chronic health condition.

💚

Mental Health & SUD

Individuals with a serious mental illness or substance use disorder diagnosis.

Must be a New York State Medicaid member with a valid Medicaid ID.
